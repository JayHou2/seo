## Day-by-day sprint

Day 1:
- Submit/request indexing for the homepage and 5 core articles.
- Post once on X or LinkedIn.
- Share with 2-3 private friends/groups for feedback.

Day 2:
- Post the New York article in one relevant community.
- Ask for missing cost categories instead of asking for praise.

Day 3:
- Post the first-month budget checklist in Chinese channels.
- Collect questions around deposits, phone cards, and insurance.

Day 4:
- Review GA4: pages, traffic source, engagement time, calculator_update events.
- Note the top 3 pages and top 3 referrers.

Day 5:
- Add one new article based on real comments.
- Improve one existing article with examples or FAQ.

Day 6:
- Post Boston or London guide to a more targeted audience.
- Do not spam broad communities; answer specific questions.

Day 7:
- Review Search Console: queries, indexed pages, impressions.
- Decide the next 5 articles based on impressions and comments.

## Metrics to watch

- GA4 Realtime: does traffic arrive after posting?
- GA4 Events: `calculator_update`, `language_switch`, `newsletter_intent`.
- Search Console Pages: which URLs are indexed.
- Search Console Performance: first queries and impressions.
- Qualitative: what questions people ask repeatedly.
